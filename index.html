Questions

Asked & answered.

Can I really use it indoors?

BRATEN produces dramatically less smoke than bottom-flame grills because fat never touches the fire. Like any gas appliance, use it in a well-ventilated space — under a range hood or with a window open. For fully enclosed spaces, always follow local regulations and the safety manual.

Where do I buy butane canisters?

The same 8-oz butane canisters used for portable camp stoves — available at grocery stores, hardware stores, camping retailers, and Amazon, usually for $2–4 each. One canister delivers roughly 1.5–2 hours of grilling.

What's the difference between PRO and CORE?

Identical grill engine. PRO adds the battery-powered auto-rotating rotisserie kit and the carry bag. If you ever want to roast whole cuts, chicken, or coffee beans, get the PRO — the rotisserie is the party trick everyone remembers.

How do I clean it?

Everything that touches food removes by hand in three steps — no tools. The cooking plates and drip tray are dishwasher-safe, or wash with regular dish soap. Total cleanup time is about five minutes.
